Investment Terms & Ownership

Ownership Structure

  • Legendary Hustlers Crew LLC is the legal entity for FaeryQuest.
  • Investors receive up to 10% equity for a $100,000 investment.
  • Option to buy back 5% equity for a $100,000 payback, investor retains 5% equity.
  • Ownership is carefully managed to ensure alignment with the vision and values of the project.

Payout & Returns

  • Investors are entitled to a share of profits proportional to their equity.
  • Buyback option allows for a handsome return on investment.
  • All payouts and buybacks are handled transparently and fairly.
  • Investors may participate in major decisions, but creative control remains with the founding team.

Protection & Flexibility

  • Terms are designed to protect both the founder and investors.
  • Contracts will be drafted to ensure clarity, fairness, and legal compliance.
  • Future upgrades, rebuilds, and expansions are planned, with ongoing opportunities for growth.

These terms are preliminary and subject to refinement. The goal is to create a win-win partnership that supports the vision and rewards early supporters.
